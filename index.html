FAQ

How quickly can you start?
We can get started as soon as you sign a consent form! Once you have uploaded a request to your board the design work will start straight away. (Mon - Sat)
What does UI mean?
UI, User Interface design, it is what you see when you open a website or a web app. The desin, look and style of the website. We design the wireframes and UI first to get your approval and then we go for production, so that lot of time, work and mistakes are saved.
How long does UI designs take to finish?
Most of the jobs are completed within 2-3 days, however, larger more complex tasks will take longer.
What are your benefits over other agencies?
We dont charge up front. We take 3 part fees. After the UI is fixed we get part 1 fees. As soon as the development is done we get the 2nd part fees, and 3rd when the site is launched.
Who are the designers?
Most of our jobs are completed by our Founder, Vishnu Gautam! 
How long does an Ecommerce project take to lanuch?
It takes 2 to 4 weeks to set up a project. Time change when we have more revisions and advanced features.
How do we communicate?
Whats app, mobile carrier, Email, Kanban board set up for your project, daily quick chats.
